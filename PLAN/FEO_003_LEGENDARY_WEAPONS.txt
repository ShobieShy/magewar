================================================================================
QUESTIONS FOR PLANNING
================================================================================

Q: Should legendary be craftable or drop-only?
   - Drop-only: More exciting
   - Craftable: More control
   - Recommendation: Drop-only with crafting alternative (hard path)

Q: How powerful should legendary be?
   - 2x regular equipment? (very powerful)
   - 1.5x regular equipment? (moderate)
   - Unique perks but not stat advantage? (interesting)
   - Recommendation: 1.5x stats + unique perks

Q: How many legendary weapons in game?
   - 5 (one per enemy type)?
   - 10 (more variety)?
   - 20+ (lots of goals)?
   - Recommendation: 8-10 (good variety, achievable)

Q: Should legendary be unique per player?
   - Only one copy in world?
   - Unlimited drops?
   - Trade-able?
   - Recommendation: Unlimited, unique, not tradeable

Q: Guaranteed drop or RNG?
   - Guaranteed from final boss?
   - Guaranteed after N boss kills?
   - Pure RNG?
   - Recommendation: Guaranteed final boss, RNG others
